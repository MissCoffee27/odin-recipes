Recipe for making rice and black beans

A simple way of cooking tasteful rice and black beans for a 3-4 person serving.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of White Rice
  • 2 cups of Water
  • 15oz can of Goya Black Beans
  • Canola/Olive Oil
  • Chopped Onions
  • Chopped Garlic
  • 1 tablespoon of Goya Sofrito

Steps

  1. Rinse 1 cup of White Rice before putting it in a pot. Pour in 2 cups of Water and cover the pot with aluminum foil in addition of the pot cover. Put on low heat at 3. Stir the rice from time to time until fully cooked. Should take about 15-20 minutes.
  2. Get another pot for the Black Beans and put a little bit of Canola/Olive Oil in the pot. Put mince Garlic, mince Onions and 1 tablespoon of Goya Sofrito inside the pot. Stir all together and put on high heat until it boils. Lower the heat to mid number 3/4
  3. Put in the full can of Black Beans with a little bit of water. Stir everything together. Should take about 5-10 minutes to cook.
  4. Serve 1 cup of cooked Rice with Black Beans on top.
