Recipe for making Tomato Salad

A simple side dish of tomato salad.

Ingredients

  • Sliced Tomatos
  • Sliced Mozzarella
  • Sliced Onions
  • Canola/Olive Oil or Vinegar

Steps

  1. Slice Tomato, Mozzarella and Onions.
  2. Place sliced tomatoes on top of each sliced mozzarella.
  3. Sprinkle chopped onions on top of the tomatoes and mozzarella.
  4. Pour a little Canola/Olive Oil or Vinegar on top.
