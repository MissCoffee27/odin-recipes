Recipe for making tasteful chicken cutlets

A simple way of cooking chicken cutlets as a side dish for dinner.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Cutlets
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Italian Bread Crumbs
  • Canola Oil or Olive Oil
  • Optional: Lemons

Steps

  1. Preparation: If cooking with 4-6 Chicken Cutlets, use 1 egg and put it in a bowl. Pour a little milk into the bowl and mix well. Pour Italian Bread Crumbs into a separate bowl. Pour a little bit of Canola/Olive Oil onto a pan.
  2. Dip each Chicken Cutlet into each bowl, starting with the Egg/Milk and then the Italian Bread Crumbs.
  3. Put each Chicken Cutlet directly in the pan and put flame on Medium/Low (About 3/4 flame).
  4. Cook on each side for 7 minutes or until fully cooked.
  5. Optional: Decorate the Chicken Cutlets with Lemons
